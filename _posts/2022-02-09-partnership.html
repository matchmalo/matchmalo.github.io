What is partnership marketing ?

Partnership marketing is a form of marketing that involves two or more companies combining their strengths to achieve a specific objective for each.
In other words, they will pool their resources and skills to increase their business.

Girlboss & Uber

Girlboss is a media company that aims to help women succeed in their careers, whether as employees or entrepreneurs.

With a limited marketing budget, the startup decided to use partnership marketing as a growth lever. Hence the idea of teaming up with Uber.

The two brands created an event to put on a unique event, Uber Pitch.

It's a pitch and workshop for women entrepreneurs who have the opportunity to win $200,000 to launch their business.

Benefits for Girlboss

  • Find the funds to organise the event.
  • Create high quality content for its audience.
  • The fact that Uber (a trendy tech startup) accepted this partnership gives Girlboss real credibility.
  • Boosting its notoriety by taking advantage of Uber's audience (see the image which is a story created by Uber on Instagram).

Benefits for Uber
